Why IOR

Built for Australia's most demanding operations.

IOR has been supplying fuel to Australia's remote and regional operations since 1984. We understand the unique challenges of fuelling mining camps, construction sites, agricultural operations, and transport fleets in locations where supply chain failures aren't an option.

Our ground fuel solutions are designed to eliminate downtime, simplify procurement, and give your operation a reliable, managed fuel supply — backed by a national network and a team that knows remote Australia.

  • Managed supply with proactive monitoring and automatic replenishment
  • National network covering metropolitan, regional, and remote locations
  • Single account, single invoice for diesel, AdBlue®, and lubricants
  • Dedicated account management and 24/7 emergency support
  • Integrated with HyDip™ tank monitoring and FuelCharge app
