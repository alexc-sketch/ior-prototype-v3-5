How It Works

One ecosystem. Three layers.

1

Hardware captures the data.

HyDip™ telemetry hardware is installed on your bulk tanks and bowsers, capturing every drop of fuel stored and dispensed in real time.

2

Mobile apps authorise the transaction.

Your drivers use the cashless Fuelcharge app or encrypted RFID tags to authorise pumps at your site or across our 115+ public network.

3

The cloud consolidates the reporting.

All data feeds instantly into your IOR Customer Portal, where your finance team can download tax-compliant invoices and track per-asset consumption.
