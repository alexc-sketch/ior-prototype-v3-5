Product Range

Six categories. One supplier. Delivered to your site.

Engine Oils
Heavy-duty diesel engine oils for mining, agricultural, and transport equipment. Available in mono-grade and multi-grade formulations to suit all major OEM specifications.
Grades: 15W-40, 10W-30, SAE 40, SAE 50
Specs: API CI-4, CK-4, CH-4
Pack sizes: 20L, 60L, 205L drum, bulk
Enquire →
Hydraulic Fluids
Anti-wear hydraulic oils for excavators, loaders, and hydraulic systems operating in extreme temperature ranges. Formulated for long service life and system protection.
Grades: ISO VG 32, 46, 68, 100
Specs: DIN 51524 Part 2 (HLP)
Pack sizes: 20L, 205L drum, IBC, bulk
Enquire →
Greases
Lithium complex and calcium sulphonate greases for heavy-duty bearing and chassis lubrication. Formulated for high-temperature, high-load, and water-washout resistance.
Types: Lithium Complex, Calcium Sulphonate
Grades: NLGI 1, 2, 3
Pack sizes: 500g cartridge, 15kg, 50kg pail
Enquire →
Gear Oils
EP gear oils for differentials, gearboxes, and final drives on mining and agricultural equipment. Extreme pressure additives protect against shock loading and micropitting.
Grades: 80W-90, 85W-140, SAE 90, SAE 140
Specs: API GL-4, GL-5
Pack sizes: 20L, 205L drum
Enquire →
Coolants
Heavy-duty extended life coolants (ELC) for diesel engines operating in extreme heat. OAT and HOAT formulations for all major engine manufacturers. Concentrate and pre-mixed available.
Types: OAT, HOAT (Extended Life)
Formats: Concentrate, 50:50 Pre-mixed
Pack sizes: 20L, 205L drum
Enquire →
Specialty Products
Compressor oils, cutting fluids, chain oils, and specialty lubricants for specific industrial applications. Speak to our lubrication specialist for a tailored recommendation.
Includes: Compressor, Chain, Cutting, Wire Rope
Applications: Mining, Processing, Industrial
Pack sizes: Various
Enquire →
