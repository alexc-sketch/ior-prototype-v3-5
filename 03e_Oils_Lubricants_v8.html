Product Range

The right lubricant for every application.

IOR supplies a comprehensive range of premium lubricants for all heavy-duty applications. Our range covers everything from engine oils to speciality greases, all available for co-delivery with your diesel supply.

Engine Oils

Premium mineral and synthetic engine oils for diesel and petrol engines. Suitable for on-highway, off-highway, and stationary applications across all major OEM specifications.

Heavy-duty diesel engine oils (HDEO) Mining and construction equipment oils Agricultural engine oils Natural gas engine oils

Hydraulic Fluids

High-performance hydraulic fluids for mobile and stationary hydraulic systems. Formulated for extreme pressure, anti-wear, and oxidation stability in demanding environments.

Anti-wear hydraulic oils (AW46, AW68) High-viscosity index (HVI) hydraulic fluids Biodegradable hydraulic fluids Fire-resistant hydraulic fluids

Greases & Specialty Lubricants

A full range of greases and specialty lubricants for mining, construction, and agricultural equipment. From open gear compounds to high-temperature greases, we have your application covered.

Lithium complex multi-purpose greases Open gear and wire rope lubricants High-temperature and EP greases Gear oils and transmission fluids

Simplified Supply

One supplier. One invoice. Everything delivered together.

IOR's oils and lubricants can be co-delivered with your diesel supply, simplifying your procurement and reducing the number of suppliers you need to manage. One account, one invoice, one delivery — covering all your fuel and lubricant needs.

  • Co-delivery with diesel to reduce transport and admin costs
  • Single account and invoice for fuel and lubricants
  • Consolidated reporting via the IOR Customer Portal
  • Drums, IBCs, and bulk formats available to match your storage
  • Dedicated account manager to manage your full product range
