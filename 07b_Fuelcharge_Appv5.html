HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to pay for fuel.

Pull up to the pump. Open Fuelcharge on your phone. Scan the QR code on the bowser.

Choose your pump number and fuel type. Fill up as much as you need — fill the tank or set a specific dollar amount.

Authorise payment in the app via credit card or IOR fleet account. Your receipt is sent instantly to your phone and email.

Frequently asked questions

All receipts are stored in the Fuelcharge app under Transaction History. You can email individual receipts or export a CSV of all transactions for a selected date range directly from the app.

Fuelcharge is available at a growing number of IOR Aviation sites. Use the in-app map or the Find a Location page to check which sites are Fuelcharge-enabled before your flight.

Fuelcharge accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Apple Pay/Google Pay. IOR fleet account holders can also charge transactions directly to their account for consolidated monthly billing.

Fleet managers can set per-driver, per-day, or per-fill spend limits directly from the Fuelcharge Fleet Dashboard. Limits take effect immediately and can be adjusted at any time.
