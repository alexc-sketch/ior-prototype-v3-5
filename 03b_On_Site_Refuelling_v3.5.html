Why On-Site Refuelling with IOR

Eight reasons operators choose IOR for on-site fuel.

Always On (24/7)

Your operation doesn’t stop at 5pm and neither does IOR. Automated dispensing means your crew can refuel at any hour without waiting for a delivery or a driver.

Fuel Made Simple (HyDip™)

Our proprietary fuel management system gives you real-time tank levels, transaction data, and secure dispensing from any device. No manual dipping, no guesswork.

Vendor-Managed Inventory

IOR monitors your tank levels remotely and schedules deliveries automatically. You never have to think about fuel ordering again.

Lower Costs

Eliminate the cost of sending vehicles to a service station. Reduce fuel theft with secure dispensing. Consolidated invoicing eliminates receipt reconciliation.

Flexible Solutions

From a 13,000L self-bunded tank for a small depot to a 100,000L installation for a major mine site. IOR scales with your operation.

Smarter Management

Full transaction history, driver-level reporting, and anomaly alerts. Know exactly who took fuel, when, and how much.

Connected on the Road

Your drivers can use their IOR tag at any of the 115+ Diesel Network stops when they’re away from the depot. One account, one tag, everywhere.

Proven & Reliable (40+ Years)

IOR has been fuelling Australian industry since 1984. We’ve operated in the harshest conditions on the continent. We know what reliable supply looks like.
