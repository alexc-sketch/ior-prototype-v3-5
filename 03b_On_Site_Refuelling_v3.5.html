Value

Fuel your fleet. Never leave the yard.

Fuel your entire fleet on-site. No drivers leaving the yard to find a diesel stop. No third-party fuel cards. No manual dipping. Total control over your fuel inventory with HyDip™ FMS monitoring every litre in real time.

No Third-Party CardsTotal Inventory ControlReal-Time Monitoring
What

Complete infrastructure. Designed and installed by IOR.

IOR designs, supplies, installs, and maintains complete on-site refuelling infrastructure. From single self-bunded bowsers to multi-product systems with automated dispensing, wet hosing capability, and full HyDip™ FMS integration for real-time tank monitoring and automated reorder.

Who

High-volume operations that can’t afford downtime.

Mining operators, construction companies, agricultural businesses, and large transport fleets that require high-volume, on-site fuel access. If your operation runs multiple vehicles or equipment from a central yard or remote site, IOR on-site refuelling eliminates the logistics of off-site fuelling entirely.
