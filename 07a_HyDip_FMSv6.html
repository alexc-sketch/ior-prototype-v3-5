Fuel management shouldn't be a guessing game.

Stop relying on manual dipsticks, estimated consumption, and missing dockets. HyDip™ replaces guesswork with precision hardware and cloud-based software, giving you absolute control over who uses your fuel, when, and where.

A complete fuel ecosystem.

As a whole system, or installed as components, HyDip® technology simplifies the way your business manages fuel.

24/7 visibility of fuel levels.

Real-time dip measurements for fuel storage tanks in any location. Remotely check tank levels, verify vendor deliveries, and automate your reordering via the HyDip Cloud platform to prevent costly stockouts.

Know exactly who is using fuel.

Deploy HyDip Fuel Tracking devices to monitor fuel usage by individual vehicle and employee using RFID tags. Spot theft early, reduce fuel loss, and generate reliable data in real-time.

Control access to fuel 24/7.

Highly customisable dispensers allow administrators to activate the bowser onsite or remotely. Built with robust stainless steel technology, it's reliable in all conditions with no printers or card swipes to take your site offline.

The fuel network manager for unattended sites.

Allow fuel transactions to occur 24/7 with no personnel required. Accept payments via the Fuelcharge app, central touch-screen terminals, or issue PIN-protected tags to authorised local account customers.

Log in to the cloud dashboard to manage tags, set limits, and instantly connect your data to Xero, MYOB, or SAP.
