The Problem

Unmanned fuel sites shouldn’t mean lost revenue.

If your fuel site isn’t staffed, you’re turning away customers — and leaving money on the table. Fuelcharge changes that by enabling cashless, self-service fuel payments at any time of day or night.

🚫
Lost revenue
Customers arrive outside staffed hours and leave without fuelling — taking their business elsewhere.
💰
Cash handling risk
Cash payments create security risks, reconciliation errors, and administrative burden for your team.
📊
No visibility
Without digital payment records, you have no visibility over site revenue, usage patterns, or customer behaviour.
The Solution

Everything your customers need to fuel up — without you being there.

Customers pay for fuel via the Fuelcharge app.

Customers download the free Fuelcharge app, select their fuel type and quantity, and pay via credit card or saved payment method — all from their smartphone.

  • Free download on iOS and Android
  • Credit card, debit card, and Apple Pay / Google Pay
  • Select fuel type and quantity before dispensing
  • No card reader or POS terminal required at site
  • Works at any Fuelcharge-enabled IOR site

Real-time revenue reporting for site operators.

Site operators get a real-time dashboard showing all Fuelcharge transactions — by site, by fuel type, by time period, and by customer.

  • Real-time transaction dashboard for site operators
  • Revenue reporting by site, fuel type, and time period
  • Customer transaction history and usage patterns
  • Automated daily/weekly revenue summaries via email
  • Export to CSV for accounting and reconciliation

Fuelcharge + HyDip: the complete unmanned fuel site solution.

Fuelcharge integrates with HyDip FMS to provide a complete unmanned fuel site solution — combining cashless payments with near real-time tank monitoring and dispensing authorisation.

  • Fuelcharge payment triggers HyDip dispensing authorisation
  • HyDip tank levels visible in the Fuelcharge operator dashboard
  • Automated low-level alerts trigger reorder via IOR
  • Complete audit trail: payment + dispensing + tank level
  • Single dashboard for both Fuelcharge and HyDip data

Frequently Asked Questions

Fuelcharge App — common questions.

Fuelcharge is a mobile app that enables customers to pay for fuel at unmanned IOR sites using their smartphone. Customers download the free app, select their fuel type and quantity, and pay via credit card or saved payment method — without needing staff on site.

Fuelcharge is suitable for any site that wants to offer cashless, self-service fuel payments — including marinas, agricultural sites, remote fuel stops, and industrial sites. It is particularly well-suited to unmanned or part-time staffed sites where customers may arrive outside business hours.

Yes. Fuelcharge requires the installation of Fuelcharge-compatible hardware at the site — connecting the fuel dispensing equipment to the Fuelcharge payment system. IOR’s team will assess your site and manage the installation process.

Fuelcharge integrates with HyDip FMS to provide a complete unmanned fuel site solution. A Fuelcharge payment triggers HyDip dispensing authorisation, and HyDip tank levels are visible in the Fuelcharge operator dashboard. This gives site operators a single view of both payment and fuel management data.
