Our Policies

HSE Policy Framework

IOR is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors. Our Occupational Health & Safety Policy establishes the framework for identifying, assessing, and controlling workplace hazards across all operations. We hold certification to ISO 45001:2018 and AS/NZS 4801:2001, verified by QAS International, demonstrating our commitment to the highest standards of occupational health and safety management.

IOR’s Environmental Management Policy reflects our commitment to minimising environmental impact across all aspects of our operations. Our policy covers spill prevention, emissions monitoring, waste management, and environmental risk assessment across all 115+ sites.
