Frequently Asked Questions

IOR Diesel Network — common questions.

The IOR Diesel Network is a national network of 115+ automated diesel stops positioned along Australia’s major transport corridors. Each stop is RFID-enabled and accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with no attendant required. Account holders receive a consolidated monthly invoice covering all network usage.

The IOR Diesel Network is available to any commercial operator with an approved IOR account. This includes transport and logistics companies, agricultural operators, mining contractors, construction fleets, and any business that requires reliable diesel access across regional and remote Australia.

Use the IOR location finder at ior.com.au/find-a-location to search for diesel stops by state, region, or postcode. The full network directory is also available as a free PDF download above.
