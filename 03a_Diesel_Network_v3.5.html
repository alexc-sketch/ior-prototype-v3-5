Frequently Asked Questions

IOR Diesel Network — common questions.

The IOR Diesel Network is Australia’s largest unmanned diesel stop network, with 115+ locations across major transport corridors. Drivers use an RFID tag to fuel 24/7 without cash or receipts. All transactions are consolidated into a single monthly invoice.

Any business with a registered IOR account can access the Diesel Network. It is particularly suited to transport operators, fleet managers, and owner-drivers running regular interstate or regional routes. Mining, civil, and agricultural operators also use the network for over-the-road fuelling.

Use the interactive network map on this page to search by state, corridor, or postcode. The full IOR Diesel Network Directory (downloadable above) lists all locations with GPS coordinates, facilities, and access details.
