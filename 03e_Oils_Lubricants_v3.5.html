Product Range

The right lubricant for every application.

IOR supplies a comprehensive range of premium lubricants for mining, agriculture, civil construction, and transport. All products are available in drum, IBC, and bulk formats, delivered direct to site.

Engine Oils

Premium diesel engine oils for heavy-duty mining, agricultural, and transport applications. API and OEM-approved grades available.

  • Heavy-duty diesel engine oils (HDEO)
  • API CK-4 and FA-4 grades
  • OEM-approved formulations
Enquire about engine oils »

Hydraulic Fluids

Anti-wear hydraulic fluids for mobile and stationary equipment. Suitable for extreme pressure, high-temperature, and fire-resistant applications.

  • Anti-wear hydraulic oils (AW46, AW68)
  • Biodegradable options available
  • Mining and civil equipment grades
Enquire about hydraulic fluids »

Greases

High-performance greases for bearings, chassis, and extreme-pressure applications in mining, agriculture, and civil construction.

  • EP lithium complex greases
  • Calcium sulphonate complex greases
  • Drum, bucket, and cartridge formats
Enquire about greases »

Gear & Transmission Oils

Gear oils, automatic transmission fluids, and wet brake fluids for heavy equipment, agricultural machinery, and transport fleets.

  • EP gear oils (GL-4, GL-5)
  • Automatic transmission fluids (ATF)
  • Wet brake and UTTO fluids
Enquire about gear oils »

Coolants & Antifreeze

Extended life coolants and antifreeze for heavy-duty diesel engines. OAT, HOAT, and conventional formulations for all climate conditions.

  • Extended life coolants (OAT)
  • Concentrate and pre-mixed options
  • Suitable for extreme temperatures
Enquire about coolants »

Specialty Lubricants

Compressor oils, chain oils, cutting fluids, and specialty lubricants for unique applications in mining, processing, and manufacturing.

  • Compressor and vacuum pump oils
  • Chain and open gear lubricants
  • Application-specific formulations
Enquire about specialty lubes »

Delivery Options

Delivered to site, on your schedule.

IOR delivers lubricants in the format you need -- from 20L pails to bulk tanker -- direct to your site. The same logistics network that delivers your diesel delivers your lubes.

Drum & Pail Delivery

20L pails, 60L pails, and 205L drums delivered to site. Palletised for easy handling.

IBC Delivery

1,000L IBCs for high-volume operations. Forklift-compatible and stackable.

Consolidated Invoicing

Diesel and lubricants on one IOR invoice. One supplier, one account, one relationship.
