FAQs

HyBlue™ AdBlue® — common questions.

Yes. HyBlue™ is ISO 22241 certified and VDA licensed — the two key international standards for diesel exhaust fluid quality. Every batch is purity tested to ensure it meets the 32.5% urea solution specification required by Euro 5 and Euro 6 SCR systems.

Using non-certified or contaminated DEF can damage your SCR system and catalytic converter — repairs can cost tens of thousands of dollars. It can also void your engine warranty. Always use ISO 22241 certified product like HyBlue™.

Yes. IOR can coordinate HyBlue™ delivery alongside your bulk diesel supply. IBCs and bulk AdBlue® can be included in your regular delivery schedule — one supplier, one invoice.

Yes. AdBlue® has a shelf life of approximately 12 months when stored correctly — away from direct sunlight, in a temperature range of -11°C to +25°C. IBCs and bulk tanks should be kept covered and away from contamination sources. HyDip™ FMS can help you manage stock rotation automatically.
