OUR DIGITAL PLATFORMS

Two platforms. One connected ecosystem.

HyDip™ and the Customer Portal solve different problems — but they work together seamlessly to give you complete control of your fuel and fleet.

HyDip Dashboard Screenshot

HyDip™ FMS

Fuel Management System

Complete fuel management for bulk storage and fleet operations. Real-time tank monitoring, per-asset fuel tracking, automated Fuel Tax Credit reporting, and seamless accounting integration — all in one cloud platform.

  • Real-time tank level monitoring
  • Per-vehicle fuel tracking & RFID authorisation
  • Automated FTC claim generation
  • Xero, MYOB & SAP integration

Customer Portal

Account Self-Service Portal

Every IOR customer gets a portal account. Manage your own logins and users, access invoices and statements, track deliveries, and control RFID fuel tags — 24/7, from any device.

  • Invoices, statements & delivery documentation
  • RFID tag management & fraud prevention
  • Fuel consumption reporting
  • Manage your own users & permissions

Fuelcharge App

Looking for a pay-as-you-go refuelling solution?

The Fuelcharge App lets you scan-and-pay at any IOR Diesel Network stop — no account required. Perfect for rural drivers, owner-operators, and small businesses that need flexibility without the commitment.
