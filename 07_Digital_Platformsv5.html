FAQS

Frequently asked questions

IOR offers three integrated digital platforms: HyDip™ FMS for real-time hardware fuel management and FTC reporting, the IOR Customer Portal for account and fleet management, and the Fuelcharge app for pay-at-the-pump transactions with no account required.
HyDip™ FMS is IOR's proprietary hardware and software fuel management system. It connects directly to your bulk fuel tanks, providing real-time level monitoring, automated reorder alerts, and ATO-ready Fuel Tax Credit reporting — with no manual data entry required.
Fuelcharge lets you find nearby IOR diesel stops and aviation fuel sites, unlock pumps remotely, and pay for fuel directly from your phone — no IOR fleet account required. Every transaction at an IOR diesel stop auto-posts to HyDip™ for customers who have both platforms.
The IOR Customer Portal is available to all IOR account holders and provides 24/7 access to invoice management, bulk fuel ordering, fleet spend tracking by vehicle or cost centre, and fuel tag management. It integrates with Xero, MYOB, and SAP for one-click accounting export.
IOR's digital tools are designed for fleet operators, transport companies, mining and resources businesses, aviation operators, and any organisation that needs accurate fuel cost tracking and compliance reporting. The Fuelcharge app is also suitable for individual drivers and pilots who need pay-at-the-pump convenience without a fleet account.
If you have bulk fuel tanks on-site and need real-time telemetry and FTC automation, HyDip™ FMS is the right fit. If you're an IOR account holder managing invoices, fleet cards, and spend across multiple sites, the Customer Portal is your primary tool. If you need a simple, no-account pay-at-pump solution for drivers or pilots, Fuelcharge is the answer. Many IOR customers use all three together for end-to-end fuel visibility.
