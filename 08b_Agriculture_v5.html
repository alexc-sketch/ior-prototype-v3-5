Frequently Asked Questions

What types of fuel does IOR supply for agriculture?

IOR supplies a comprehensive range of fuels, including diesel (AGO), unleaded petrol, and specialty blends, all meeting Australian quality standards. We can tailor fuel types to your specific machinery and operational requirements.

Can IOR help with on-farm fuel storage?

Absolutely. We offer a variety of on-farm fuel storage solutions, from self-bunded tanks to custom-designed systems, ensuring safe, compliant, and efficient storage that meets environmental regulations and your operational needs.

How does IOR ensure timely fuel delivery to remote farms?

IOR has an extensive logistics network and strategically located depots across regional Australia. Our advanced scheduling and fleet management systems, combined with experienced drivers, enable us to provide reliable and timely deliveries even to the most remote agricultural locations.

What are the benefits of using IOR's lubricants?

IOR's premium lubricants are formulated to protect your valuable agricultural machinery from wear and tear, reduce friction, and optimize performance. This leads to extended equipment life, reduced maintenance costs, and improved operational efficiency, especially in demanding farming conditions.
