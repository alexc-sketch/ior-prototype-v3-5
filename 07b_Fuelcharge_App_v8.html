HOW IT WORKS

Start using Fuelcharge in three steps

No credit application. No waiting for approval. Download the app and you’re ready to refuel at any IOR stop across Australia.

1
Download the app
Fuelcharge is available free on the App Store and Google Play. Create your account in under two minutes — no paperwork, no credit check.
2
Find your nearest IOR stop
Use the in-app map to locate diesel stops and aviation airstrips across the IOR network. Filter by fuel type — road diesel or aviation.
3
Scan, pay, and pump
Scan the QR code at the pump, confirm your litres, and pay securely from your phone. Your digital receipt is stored instantly in the app.
