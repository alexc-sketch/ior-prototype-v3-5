HOW IT WORKS

Start using Fuelcharge in three steps

No paperwork. No waiting for approval. Download the app and you’re ready to refuel.

1
Download the app
Fuelcharge is available free on the App Store and Google Play. Create your account in under two minutes and add your payment details.
2
Select the fuel
Use the in-app map to locate diesel stops and aviation airstrips. Select your fuel type and pre-authorise your payment. You won’t be charged for fuel you don’t receive.
3
Pay & Fill up
Scan the QR code at the pump, confirm your litres, and fill up. Your digital receipt is stored instantly in the app. No paper receipts, no credit card machines, no worries.

Fuelcharge is accepted at over 110 IOR diesel stops and more than 30 aviation airstrips across Australia. You can use the map inside the app to find your nearest location and check current pricing.
Before you pump, you select a pre-authorised amount in the app (e.g., $150). This reserves the funds on your card. You then fill your tank, and you will only be charged for the exact amount of fuel you actually receive.
Depending on the location, you can use Fuelcharge to purchase Diesel, AdBlue®, Avgas, and Jet A-1.
