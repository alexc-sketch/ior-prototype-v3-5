We're Here to Help

With over 40 years of industry experience, IOR proudly operates more than 115 network sites across Australia, including 110 diesel stops. We are an Australian-operated fuel company committed to delivering reliable service and innovative solutions to enterprise clients nationwide.

Whether you have commercial enquiries or need support, our dedicated team is ready to assist you. We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day.

IOR has been fuelling regional and remote Australia since 1984. Our team understands the unique demands of operating in remote and regional environments, and we are equipped to provide tailored solutions for your operation.
