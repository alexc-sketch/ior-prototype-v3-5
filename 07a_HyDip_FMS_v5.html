HyDip™ Fuel Management System

Take control of your fuel network.

HyDip™ is IOR’s proprietary cloud-based fuel management system. Near real-time tank monitoring, usage tracking, dispensing control, and automated reordering.

Fuel management shouldn’t be a guessing game.

Manual dips. Spreadsheets. Unexpected runouts. Unauthorised usage. For operators of remote and unattended fuel sites, managing fuel has always been reactive — not proactive. HyDip changes that.

Supported. Robust. Connected.

Local Assistance. National Coverage.

24/7 local assistance and national coverage across Australia’s most remote and regional fuel sites.

Reliable in All Conditions.

Resilient to power and communication fluctuations. HyDip keeps working when other systems go offline.

One Platform. Every Asset.

Your entire network of assets on one platform, accessible via web, mobile, and tablet from anywhere with internet coverage.

The Four Pillars

Intuitive, cloud-based system designed in Australia

Gauge

Real-time tank monitoring from anywhere.

Real-time dip measurements of storage tanks in any location with internet coverage. Monitor levels remotely via the HyDip Cloud platform and receive automatic alerts when tanks need topping up.

Get a Quote Download Brochure
Track

RFID-powered usage tracking by individual user.

RFID devices monitor usage and report reliable data in real-time by individual user. Spot theft and tag misuse early, and generate detailed reports to track fuel usage across your fleet.

Get a Quote Download Brochure
Dispense

Activate the bowser onsite or remotely.

Customisable dispenser allows administrators to activate the bowser onsite or remotely. Robust technology suitable for any location, with no printers or card swipes to take your site offline.

Get a Quote Download Brochure
Pay

Unmanned transactions with multiple payment options.

Enables unmanned transactions with multiple payment facilities, including the Fuelcharge app. Issue PIN-protected tags to authorised local account customers for secure access to fuel.
