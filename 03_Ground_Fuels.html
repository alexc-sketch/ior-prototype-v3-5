Frequently Asked Questions

Ground Fuel Solutions — common questions.

What fuel solutions does IOR offer?

IOR offers a comprehensive suite of ground fuel solutions including the IOR Diesel Network (115+ unmanned stops), on-site refuelling with IOR-owned infrastructure, bulk diesel delivery, HyBlue™ AdBlue®, and Caltex/Delo oils and lubricants. All solutions can be managed through a single IOR account.

Can IOR supply both diesel and aviation fuel?

Yes. IOR operates across both ground fuels and aviation, supplying Jet A-1 and Avgas through the IOR Aviation Network and airport fuelling services. A single IOR account can cover both ground and aviation fuel requirements.

Do you provide bulk fuel delivery and on-site refuelling?

Yes. IOR provides both bulk diesel delivery (by rigid tanker, semi, or road train) and on-site refuelling with IOR-owned self-bunded tanks. Both services can be combined and managed under a single vendor-managed replenishment arrangement.

Can IOR help with fuel storage and infrastructure?

Yes. IOR supplies and installs self-bunded fuel storage tanks (T13 to T100 range), frac tanks for temporary storage, and on-site refuelling infrastructure. All tanks are AS 1940 compliant and can be integrated with HyDip™ FMS for live monitoring.

What industries use IOR ground fuel solutions?

IOR serves transport and logistics, mining and resources, agriculture, civil construction, and aviation. Solutions are tailored to the specific access, volume, and compliance requirements of each industry.

How do I choose the right fuel solution for my business?

The right solution depends on your volume, site access, and operational requirements. Use the spoke cards above to explore each option, or contact an IOR fuel specialist for a tailored recommendation. Most customers combine two or more solutions under a single account.
