Investing in the communities we serve

IOR's roots are in regional Australia. We give back to the towns and communities that have supported our growth for over 40 years.

Our Commitment

Community is at the heart of everything we do

Since our founding in 1984, IOR has operated in and alongside the regional communities of Australia. We understand that our success is inseparable from the health and prosperity of the towns, industries, and people we serve. That is why we invest in meaningful, long-term community partnerships across sport, education, emergency services, and social welfare.

How We Give Back

Our Community Pillars

Regional Sport & Recreation
IOR sponsors local sporting clubs and events across regional Queensland, New South Wales, and Western Australia, helping communities maintain the facilities and programs that bring people together.
Education & Scholarships
We support trade and vocational education pathways in the regions where we operate, providing scholarships and apprenticeship opportunities for young Australians pursuing careers in transport, engineering, and energy.
Emergency Services Support
IOR provides fuel and logistics support to volunteer fire brigades, rural ambulance services, and emergency management organisations across remote and regional Australia.
Indigenous Community Partnerships
We work with Indigenous community organisations and enterprises to create employment pathways and economic participation in the regions where IOR operates.
Social Welfare & Hardship
IOR contributes to regional food banks, welfare organisations, and hardship funds, particularly during natural disaster events affecting the communities we serve.
Environmental Stewardship
We invest in local environmental programs including land rehabilitation, waterway protection, and biodiversity initiatives in partnership with regional councils and land management bodies.
