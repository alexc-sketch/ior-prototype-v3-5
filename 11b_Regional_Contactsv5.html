Frequently Asked Questions

Select your state on the map above to view the contact cards for your region. Each card lists the representative’s name, title, phone number, email address, and service area. For general enquiries, you can also call our national line on 1300 457 467.
Many of our regional and aviation representatives are field-based and do not operate from a public-facing corporate office. Rather than listing a home address or car park, we list their Service Area — the geographic region they cover. To arrange a meeting, please contact them directly by phone or email.
For urgent after-hours supply emergencies, call our national support line on 1300 457 467. Our central team is available to assist with emergency supply situations.
Contact your local representative using the details above, or reach out to our national team via the Contact Us page. Our team will guide you through the account application process and connect you with the right fuel solution for your operation.
