Eromanga Refinery

Where the IOR story began.

In 1984, IOR started as a small oil refinery in the remote Queensland town of Eromanga — 1,000km west of Brisbane. Forty years later, we are one of Australia's largest independent fuel distributors. The refinery still runs. The crude oil is still local. The commitment hasn't changed.

Eromanga UMF
Flagship Product

Eromanga UMF — The lowest-particulate diesel mining fuel on the market.

Eromanga UMF (Underground Mining Fuel) is the primary product of the Eromanga Refinery. It is a highly specialised diesel fuel engineered for underground mining environments, where exhaust gas particulates pose a serious health and safety risk to workers. Eromanga UMF produces lower exhaust gas particulates than any other diesel fuel currently available on the Australian market.

Lowest Particulates

Engineered to produce the lowest exhaust gas particulates of any diesel fuel on the Australian market. Critical for underground mining environments where air quality directly impacts worker health and safety.

Locally Produced Crude

Refined from locally produced crude oil — not imported. This is a genuine sovereign supply product with no exposure to international shipping disruption or geopolitical risk.

Continuous Production Since 1986

The refinery has been continuously processing crude oil since it was commissioned in 1986. No gaps in production. No supply interruptions. Forty years of unbroken output.

Community

A significant contributor to far western Queensland.

Located in one of the most remote regions of Australia, the Eromanga Refinery has been a cornerstone of the local community for four decades. We provide training, employment, and business opportunities in a region where economic activity is scarce. Almost every person who has worked at this refinery has a connection to the communities of Eromanga, Quilpie, and the Channel Country.
