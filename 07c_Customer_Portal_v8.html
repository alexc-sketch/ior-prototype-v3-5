Platform Features

Everything you need to manage your fuel.

Visibility

Total network visibility.

Track bulk deliveries as they happen. Integrate with HyDip™ FMS to see real-time dips and usage directly from your bulk tank, giving you full visibility and control over what was delivered versus what was pumped.

[ UI Mockup: Tag management — active/suspended RFID toggles ]
Security

Mitigate fraud instantly.

Add, suspend, or cancel RFID fuel tags in real time, adding instant protection against unauthorised use or theft. Multiple security layers mean only authorised users access your fuel.

[ UI Mockup: Consumption report — fuel by vehicle/driver/site ]
Reporting

Understand your consumption.

Generate granular reports on all transactions. Understand exactly how much fuel is being consumed between top-ups, broken down per vehicle, per driver, or per site.

[ UI Mockup: Invoice download & live pricing chart ]
