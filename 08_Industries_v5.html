Frequently Asked Questions

What industries does IOR specialise in?
We provide dedicated solutions for Transport, Mining, Agriculture, Civil Construction, Aviation, Marine, and Government.
Do you offer national coverage for enterprise fleets?
Yes, our proprietary diesel network features over 115+ locations, supported by a massive bulk delivery fleet operating nationwide.
Can you design bespoke fuel storage for unique sites?
Absolutely. Our infrastructure team engineers and deploys custom self-bunded tanks and frac tanks for remote and highly specific operational requirements.
