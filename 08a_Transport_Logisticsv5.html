OUR SOLUTIONS

Everything your fleet needs, in one place.

IOR’s transport solutions are engineered around the operational realities of Australian freight: long distances, tight margins, and zero tolerance for downtime.

IOR Diesel Stop Network

Access 116+ proprietary diesel stops across Australia’s major freight corridors. Open 24/7, with RFID and fleet card acceptance, so your drivers never have to detour.

Bulk Diesel Delivery

Scheduled bulk delivery direct to your depot or yard. We manage the logistics so you can focus on running your fleet, not chasing fuel supply.

Fleet Cards & RFID

Fuelcharge fleet cards and RFID tags give you per-vehicle visibility, spending controls, and consolidated invoicing. Integrate with your existing fleet management software.

HyDip™ Telematics

Real-time tank monitoring and fuel usage reporting via the HyDip FMS platform. Know your fuel levels, consumption rates, and anomalies before they become problems.

HyBlue™ AdBlue

IOR’s own-brand AdBlue, available in bulk and drum formats. Compatible with all Euro 5/6 diesel engines and available at selected diesel stop locations.

Dedicated Account Management

A single point of contact who understands your routes, your fleet, and your business. IOR account managers are based in the regions they serve.
