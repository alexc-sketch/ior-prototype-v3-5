Frequently Asked Questions

Digital Platforms — common questions.

IOR offers three digital platforms: HyDip™ FMS (near real-time fuel management system for unattended sites), FuelCharge (cashless fuel payment app for drivers and operators), and the IOR Customer Portal (account management, reporting, and delivery visibility for IOR account holders).

HyDip™ FMS is IOR's near real-time fuel management system. It monitors tank levels, authorises fuel dispensing, and provides a cloud-based dashboard for complete visibility over your fuel operations. HyDip™ is designed for unattended remote sites and integrates with IOR's delivery scheduling system.

FuelCharge is IOR's cashless fuel payment app. Drivers and operators use FuelCharge to pay for fuel at any IOR diesel stop, airport, or marina — no cash, no receipts, no reimbursements. All transactions are recorded and consolidated into a single monthly invoice.

The IOR Customer Portal is a secure online account management platform for IOR account holders. It provides real-time delivery tracking, fuel usage reporting, tag management, invoice access, and pricing visibility — all in one place.

HyDip™ FMS and the Customer Portal are available to IOR account holders. FuelCharge is available to anyone — you can download the app and create an account without an existing IOR relationship. Contact IOR to set up an account and access all three platforms.

Yes. HyDip™ FMS is specifically designed for remote and unattended sites. It operates on cellular or satellite connectivity and continues to function in offline mode, syncing data when connectivity is restored.

Yes. HyDip™ FMS can be retrofitted to most existing self-bunded and above-ground fuel storage tanks. IOR's installation team will assess your existing infrastructure and advise on the retrofit process.
