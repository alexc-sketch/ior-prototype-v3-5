FAQs

Frequently asked questions about HyBlue™ AdBlue®.

AdBlue® (also known as Diesel Exhaust Fluid or DEF) is a urea-water solution used in Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems to reduce NOx emissions from diesel engines. It is required in most modern Euro 5 and Euro 6 diesel vehicles.

Yes. HyBlue™ is VDA certified and meets ISO 22241 quality standards. It is manufactured in Australia under strict quality control processes.

HyBlue™ is available at 115+ IOR Diesel Network stops, via direct delivery in 10L containers, 210L drums, 1,000L IBCs, and bulk tanker delivery. Contact IOR to set up an account.

Yes. HyBlue™ is available at IOR Diesel Network stops and can be charged to your existing IOR account. One account for diesel and AdBlue®.

Modern vehicles will reduce power or refuse to start if AdBlue® runs out. IOR's vendor-managed supply and HyDip™ monitoring can alert you before levels become critical.

HyBlue™ meets ISO 22241 and is compatible with all vehicles requiring DEF/AdBlue®. Always check your vehicle manufacturer's specifications.
