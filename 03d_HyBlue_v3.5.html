What Is HyBlue™?

Australian-made AdBlue. Certified quality.

HyBlue™ is IOR’s branded Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF), also known as AdBlue. It is required by all Euro 5, Euro 6, and Tier 4 diesel engines to reduce NOx emissions via Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) technology.

HyBlue™ is manufactured in Australia to ISO 22241 and VDA standards, ensuring consistent quality and compatibility with all SCR-equipped vehicles and equipment.

Available Pack Sizes

The right size for every operation.

10L Container
Ideal for light commercial vehicles, passenger cars, and small fleets. Easy-pour spout included. Available at IOR diesel stops.
210L Drum
For medium fleets and regular consumers. Pump dispensing recommended. Delivered direct to site or available at selected IOR stops.
1,000L IBC
Intermediate Bulk Container for high-volume operations. Forklift-compatible. Delivered direct to site by IOR.
Bulk Delivery
For large mining, agricultural, and transport operations. Bulk HyBlue™ delivered direct to your site tank. Automated reorder available via HyDip™ FMS.
Why HyBlue™?

Same supplier. One invoice.

Source your AdBlue from the same supplier as your diesel. One IOR account, one invoice, one point of contact. No separate AdBlue supplier to manage.

HyBlue™ is available at IOR diesel stops across Australia, so your drivers can top up AdBlue at the same stop as diesel — no detour required.
