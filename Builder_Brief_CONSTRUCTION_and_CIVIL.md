8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Q1: What is wet-hire refuelling?
A: Wet-hire (our X2U service) involves an IOR tanker driving directly onto your civil site and dispensing fuel straight into your machinery and equipment, saving your operators time.
Q2: Do we have to pay to move tanks as our pipeline/road project progresses?
A: No. While we can relocate tanks, you can also simply utilise our 115+ public network sites via RFID tags as your project footprint moves.
Q3: Are your tanks compliant for civil construction sites?
A: Yes, all our self-bunded tanks and frac tanks are manufactured to meet strict AS1692 and AS1940 compliance standards.
Q4: Can we track fuel usage by specific project or machine?
A: Yes, HyDip™ FMS and our Customer Portal provide granular reporting, allowing you to accurately assign fuel costs to specific project cost centres.
Q5: How fast can you deploy a temporary tank to our site?
A: Contact our infrastructure team today. We have a massive fleet of deployable assets ready to move to your site as quickly as project approvals allow.
