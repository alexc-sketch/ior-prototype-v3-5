School-Based Trainees

School-Based Traineeships at IOR

Earn While You Learn — Start Your Fuel Industry Career at School

[IMAGE — School-based trainee photography]
Home › Careers › Entry Pathways › School-Based Trainees

IOR’s school-based traineeship program gives students the opportunity to earn a nationally recognised qualification while completing their secondary education. Working alongside experienced IOR team members, trainees gain real-world experience in fuel distribution, logistics, and infrastructure operations.

Our program is structured to fit around your school commitments — with flexible scheduling and dedicated supervisor support throughout.

IOR has been fuelling regional and remote Australia since 1984, and we believe the best careers start early.

Program Details

What You Need to Know

Who Can Apply
Year 10, 11, and 12 students in Queensland and regional Australia
What You’ll Earn
A nationally recognised Certificate II or III qualification in your chosen stream
How It Works
Structured on-the-job training, 1–2 days per week, alongside your school timetable

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
