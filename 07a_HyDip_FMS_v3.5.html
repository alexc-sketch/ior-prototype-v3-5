The Problem

Fuel management shouldn’t be a guessing game.

Manual dips. Spreadsheets. Unexpected runouts. Unauthorised usage. For operators of remote and unattended fuel sites, managing fuel has always been reactive — not proactive. HyDip changes that.

📊
No visibility
You don’t know how much fuel is in your tanks until someone physically checks — or you run out.
🔒
No control
You can’t control who accesses your fuel, when, or how much — leaving you exposed to theft and unauthorised use.
⏰
No time
Manual reporting, reconciliation, and ordering takes time you don’t have — and creates errors you can’t afford.
The Solution

Everything you need to manage your fuel network — from one dashboard.

See inside all your tanks — in near real time.

HyDip’s map-based dashboard gives you a live view of tank levels across your entire fuel network — from a single screen, from anywhere.

  • Near real-time tank level monitoring via HDv2 units
  • Map-based dashboard showing all sites and tank levels
  • Automated low-level alerts and reorder notifications
  • Historical level data and trend analysis
  • Multi-site visibility from a single dashboard

Know who is using your fuel — and when.

HyDip tracks every dispensing event — by employee, vehicle, or tag — giving you complete visibility over fuel usage and the ability to build a distribution network.

  • Per-employee or per-vehicle usage tracking

  • Fraud detection and unauthorised access alerts

HFT X2 — Dispensing Authorisation Unit.

The HFT X2 is HyDip’s dispensing authorisation hardware — installed at the pump to control who can access your fuel and record every dispensing event.

  • Tag-based dispensing authorisation at the pump
  • Records volume, time, and user for every dispensing event
  • Works in remote and off-grid environments
  • Compatible with all standard fuel dispensing equipment
  • Connects to the HyDip cloud dashboard via cellular or satellite

HDv2 — Tank Monitoring Unit.

The HDv2 is HyDip’s tank monitoring hardware — installed on your tank to provide near real-time level data to the HyDip cloud dashboard.

  • Near real-time tank level monitoring via ultrasonic sensor
  • Works on above-ground and underground tanks
  • Battery-powered — no mains power required
  • Connects to the HyDip cloud dashboard via cellular or satellite
  • Compatible with all standard fuel storage tanks

Frequently Asked Questions

HyDip™ FMS — common questions.

HyDip™ FMS monitors fuel tank levels in near real-time, authorises fuel dispensing via RFID tag or PIN, records all transactions per user and vehicle, and provides a cloud-based dashboard for complete operational visibility. It also integrates with IOR’s delivery scheduling system to automate replenishment.

Yes. HyDip™ FMS is specifically designed for remote and unattended sites. It operates on cellular or satellite connectivity and continues to function in offline mode, syncing data when connectivity is restored.

Yes. HyDip™ FMS can be retrofitted to most existing self-bunded and above-ground fuel storage tanks. IOR’s installation team will assess your existing infrastructure and advise on the retrofit process.

HyDip™ FMS requires RFID tag or PIN authorisation before dispensing fuel. All transactions are recorded per user and vehicle registration. Unauthorised dispensing attempts are logged and can trigger alerts. This provides a complete audit trail and deters theft at unattended sites.
