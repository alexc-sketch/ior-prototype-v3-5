Use the filter panel on the left side of the map to select “Truck Wash” under the Amenities category. The map will update to show only sites with truck wash facilities. You can combine multiple filters — for example, Truck Wash + B-Double Access — to narrow your search further.

The map reflects our current network of active sites. In the event of a temporary outage or planned maintenance, affected sites may be marked accordingly. For real-time status, IOR account holders can check site availability through the Customer Portal or contact our team on 1300 457 467.
