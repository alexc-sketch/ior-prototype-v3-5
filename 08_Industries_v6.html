WHY IOR

One supplier. Every industry. No compromises.

IOR is Australia's only independent fuel specialist with the infrastructure, digital platforms, and operational expertise to serve every major industry sector. We own our supply chain from terminal to tank — which means we control quality, availability, and pricing at every step.

01

We own our infrastructure

Import terminals, regional depots, diesel stops, frac tanks, and X2U units — IOR owns and operates the infrastructure that keeps your operation running. No third-party dependencies.

02

We go where others won't

87% of IOR's sales volume is outside major cities. Our 17 regional depots and road train delivery capability mean we can reach remote sites that are beyond the range of any other commercial fuel supplier in Australia.

03

We are Australia's sovereign fuel supplier

In April 2026, IOR entered a commercial framework with the Federal Government and Export Finance Australia as Australia's designated sovereign fuel supplier — the only Australian-owned, Australian-operated fuel company with the infrastructure to supply the nation's critical operations.
