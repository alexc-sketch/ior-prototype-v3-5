FAQS

Common questions from oil & gas operators.

Yes, we specialise in remote logistics and bulk delivery to the harshest regions, tracking your camps as they move. IOR operates its own fleet of modern tankers and has established supply routes to remote sites across the Cooper Basin, Bowen Basin, and other major upstream regions.
Yes, we supply high-capacity frac tanks (450–500 bbl) specifically engineered for major resource projects. All IOR deployable frac tanks are AS1940-compliant for the storage and handling of flammable and combustible liquids. We provide full compliance documentation and can assist with site-specific risk assessments on request.
We use HyDip™ FMS for live tank monitoring, which triggers automated supply scheduling from our logistics team. When levels drop below a configurable threshold, the system sends automated alerts to both the site operator and IOR's supply team, enabling proactive resupply scheduling before the site runs dry.
Yes, we operate under strict HSE frameworks and hold ISO 14001 certification for Environmental Management. All IOR fuel supply and infrastructure services include full HSE documentation, Safety Data Sheets, delivery dockets, and consumption reports suitable for site safety management systems and regulatory compliance.
IOR owns and operates its entire supply chain — from our inland Eromanga Refinery and coastal import terminals through to our own transport fleet. This means we are not dependent on third-party suppliers and can guarantee continuity of supply even during market disruptions. We are a sovereign supply partner, not a middleman.
