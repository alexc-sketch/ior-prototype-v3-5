OUR SOLUTIONS

Fuel solutions for government and defense.

IOR provides compliant, reliable fuel supply for government fleets, defense facilities, emergency services, and public sector operations across Australia.

Government Fleet Fuelling

IOR provides bulk diesel, unleaded, and AdBlue for government vehicle fleets. Our Fuelcharge fleet card system provides full transaction reporting for fleet management and audit purposes.

Defense Facility Supply

IOR has experience supplying fuel to defense facilities with the security, compliance, and documentation requirements that apply. We work within your procurement and security frameworks.

Emergency Services Fuel

IOR supplies fuel to emergency services organisations including fire, police, and ambulance. We understand the critical nature of supply continuity and maintain emergency response capability.

Fuelcharge Fleet Cards

IOR’s Fuelcharge fleet card system provides full transaction visibility, cost centre reporting, and driver-level controls for government fleet managers. Accepted at 116+ IOR diesel stops nationally.

Compliance Documentation

Full compliance documentation including Safety Data Sheets, delivery dockets, and consumption reports. All documentation meets government procurement and audit requirements.

Bulk Storage Infrastructure

IOR designs, supplies, and installs bulk fuel storage for government facilities and depots. We handle compliance, installation, and ongoing maintenance under a single service agreement.
