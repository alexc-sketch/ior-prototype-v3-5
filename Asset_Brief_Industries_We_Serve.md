### Technical Requirements for All Assets
* **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (Landscape) for all hero split images.
* **Format:** WebP or highly optimized JPEG.
* **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080px for crisp display on high-DPI screens.
* **Style:** Authentic, high-fidelity, Australian context. Avoid generic stock photography where possible; prefer actual IOR assets, vehicles, and infrastructure.
