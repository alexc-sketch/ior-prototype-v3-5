Fleet & Delivery

IOR operates a dedicated aviation fuel delivery fleet capable of reaching the most remote airstrips in Australia. From small bowsers for light aircraft to high-volume road trains for bulk drops, we have the right vehicle for every location.

  • Purpose-built aviation fuel tankers for all access types
  • Small bowsers for access-restricted and smaller airstrips
  • High-volume road trains for remote bulk drops
  • Dedicated aviation-only fleet — no switch loading risk
  • Flexible delivery scheduling to match your operation
  • Emergency delivery capability for unplanned demand

Safety & Compliance

Every IOR bulk aviation delivery is conducted to JIG standards by trained operators — with full documentation and chain-of-custody records for every uplift.

  • JIG-compliant delivery procedures for all bulk aviation fuel
  • Highly trained operators certified in aviation fuel handling
  • Full chain-of-custody documentation from terminal to aircraft
  • Fuel quality certificates provided with every delivery
  • Regular third-party audits and compliance reporting
