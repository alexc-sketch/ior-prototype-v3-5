BLOCK 6 — SOLUTIONS OVERVIEW · 4-CATEGORY PROBLEM-LED LAYOUT · Client brief: "On the Ground / In the Sky / Wholesale / Specialty Products" 4 columns. Each column: category label (top) → problem statement in italic → product pills (linked to spoke pages) → "Learn more →" link. Aviation column uses gold accent (#D98300). Pills are outline style, hover fills with category colour. Headline max-width: 75% of page width. Sub-headline below grid: "Each solution works standalone — or together as a complete fuel management ecosystem." INTENT: Lead with the problem each solution solves, not just the product name. Revenue Engine self-segments by scanning category labels and clicking product pills to reach the spoke they need — without reading body copy. Do not overload with detail — enough to prompt a click-through, not answer every question.
