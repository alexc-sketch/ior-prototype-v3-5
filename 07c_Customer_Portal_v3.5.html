Portal Features

Everything you need to manage your IOR account — in one place.

Track your IOR deliveries in real time.

The Customer Portal gives you real-time visibility over all your IOR deliveries — from order placement through to delivery confirmation and tank level update.

  • Real-time delivery tracking from order to completion
  • Delivery confirmation notifications via email or SMS
  • Delivery history by site, date, and product
  • Estimated delivery windows and driver tracking
  • Delivery docket access and download

Manage your fuel tags — add, suspend, or cancel.

The Customer Portal gives you complete control over your RFID fuel tags — add new tags, suspend access, or cancel tags in real time, without contacting IOR.

  • Add new RFID fuel tags for employees or vehicles
  • Suspend tag access instantly — no waiting for IOR
  • Cancel tags permanently when no longer needed
  • View tag usage history by employee or vehicle
  • Set usage limits and alerts per tag

Understand your fuel usage — by site, vehicle, or cost centre.

The Customer Portal provides detailed usage reporting — breaking down fuel consumption by site, vehicle, employee, or cost centre, with export to CSV for accounting and reconciliation.

  • Usage reporting by site, vehicle, employee, or cost centre
  • Consumption trend analysis and benchmarking
  • Anomaly detection and unusual usage alerts
  • Export to CSV for accounting and reconciliation
  • Automated weekly and monthly usage summaries

Access invoices and current pricing — any time.

The Customer Portal gives you instant access to all your IOR invoices and current fuel pricing — so you can reconcile accounts and plan fuel budgets without contacting IOR.

  • Invoice access and download — current and historical
  • Current fuel pricing by product and site
  • Price change notifications via email
  • Budget planning tools and price forecasting
  • GST-compliant invoices for accounting integration
