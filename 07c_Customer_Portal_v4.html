Platform Features

Everything you need to manage your fuel account

Real-Time Fuel Dips

View current tank levels across all your sites in real time. No more manual dip readings or phone calls to check stock.

Usage Trends

Track daily, weekly, and monthly fuel consumption patterns. Identify anomalies and optimise your fuel ordering cycles.

Stock History

Access a full history of deliveries, dips, and transactions. Export data for reconciliation or compliance reporting.

Fleet Tag Management

Add, remove, and manage fuel tags for your entire fleet from one dashboard. Assign tags to vehicles, drivers, or cost centres.

Instant Tag Suspension

Suspend or cancel a tag immediately if a vehicle is sold or a tag is lost. No waiting for business hours.

Multi-User Access

Grant portal access to multiple team members with role-based permissions. Finance, operations, and management can each see what they need.

Transaction Reports

Download detailed transaction reports by site, vehicle, driver, or date range. Export to CSV or PDF for your accounting system.

FTC Reporting

Fuel Tax Credit reporting data is automatically compiled. Reduce manual effort and ensure your FTC claims are accurate.

Invoice Access

View and download invoices directly from the portal. No more waiting for paper statements or emailing accounts.

Fraud Prevention

Set transaction limits, site restrictions, and time-of-day controls per tag. Unusual activity triggers automatic alerts.

Secure Login

Multi-factor authentication and role-based access ensure only authorised users can view or manage your account.

Real-Time Alerts

Receive instant notifications for low stock, unusual transactions, or tag misuse. Stay in control even when you're off-site.

Why Customer Portal

Included with every IOR account

No Additional Cost

Customer Portal is included with every IOR account at no extra charge. Activate your access and start managing your fuel network today.

Works on Any Device

Access the portal from desktop, tablet, or mobile. A responsive interface means you can check tank levels from the paddock or the boardroom.

Integrates with HyDip™

If you use HyDip™ FMS, your Customer Portal data is automatically synchronised. One login, full visibility of your fuel network.

Supports Multiple Sites

Manage fuel data across multiple properties, depots, or locations from a single portal login. Ideal for large agribusiness and transport operators.

Accounting Integration

Export transaction data in formats compatible with Xero, MYOB, and other major accounting platforms. Reduce manual data entry.

IOR Support Backed

Our team is available Mon–Fri 7am–6pm and Sat 8am–1pm to assist with portal setup, tag management, and reporting queries.
