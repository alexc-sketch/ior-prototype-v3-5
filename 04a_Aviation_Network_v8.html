Why the IOR Aviation Network

Accredited supply for demanding operations.

CASA-Compliant Supply

All IOR aviation fuel supply meets CASA regulatory requirements. Our accredited operators follow strict quality control protocols at every site.

RFID Tag Technology

Secure, unmanned fuelling at select locations via RFID tag. Cashless, fast, and fully auditable — every transaction recorded to your account.

Customer Portal Visibility

Real-time transaction data, fuel uplift records, and account management — all accessible via the IOR Customer Portal, 24/7.

National Airport Coverage

From major capital city airports to remote outback aerodromes, IOR's aviation network reaches where other suppliers don't.

HOW IT WORKS

Up and running in three steps.

01

Apply for an Aviation Account

Complete the online application. Our aviation team reviews and approves most accounts within one business day.

02

Receive Your RFID Tags & Account Details

Your RFID tags are dispatched immediately on approval. Each tag is registered to your aircraft or fleet and linked to your account.

03

Fuel at Any IOR Aviation Location

Access CASA-compliant fuel at any IOR Aviation location. Transactions consolidate automatically into your monthly invoice.
