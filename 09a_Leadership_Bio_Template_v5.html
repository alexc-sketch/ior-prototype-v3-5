Daniel Roberts

General Manager, Ground Fuels

Connect on LinkedIn

Daniel is the person IOR’s Ground Fuels customers rely on to deliver. His role is straightforward in principle and demanding in practice: make sure the right fuel reaches the right customer, at the right time, every time. Across mining operations, agricultural businesses, transport fleets, and construction sites, Daniel’s team is the operational engine that keeps IOR’s ground fuels business moving.

With deep experience across fuel supply, logistics, and customer operations, Daniel brings both the technical knowledge and the customer focus that IOR’s ground fuels business demands. He understands that for many of IOR’s customers, a fuel supply failure is not an inconvenience — it is a production stoppage, a missed harvest window, or a stranded fleet. His team’s job is to make sure that never happens.

Daniel is committed to building long-term relationships with IOR’s customers, understanding their operations, and finding ways to make fuel supply simpler, more reliable, and more efficient. Under his leadership, IOR’s Ground Fuels business continues to grow its network, expand its service capabilities, and deepen its presence in the industries and communities that depend on it.
