At IOR, we offer a leading fuel industry graduate program designed for ambitious early careers professionals. As a proudly Australian, community-focused company, we provide graduates with genuine responsibility from the start — no coffee runs, just impactful work.

IOR has been fuelling regional and remote Australia since 1984, and our graduate program is how we invest in the next generation of leaders who will carry that legacy forward.
